RingRing

The Subject Alarm Application for android

Most students take more than one class But the sites are all different We are exhausted before we even begin the task. So I thought, 'How happy would it be if I could see all the tasks at once' It is an android application that can do the above. We used Firebase to solve both the DB and the server at once, and we optimized the program using design patterns such as adapters and viewholders, and different data structures.

IoT BLINO

Automatic Blind using Aduino

This product provides a service to measure the intensity of sunlight or internal temperature using an Aduino sensor and raise and lower blinds automatically. It will remove the inconvenience of opening and closing the blinds manually depending on the circumstances. and It is expected that the blind will open and close automatically according to the room temperature, which will bring the effect of the room temperature control.
